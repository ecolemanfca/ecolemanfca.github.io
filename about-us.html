The Founder

My dream has always been to bake cakes and cupcakes that will bring joy to people. Ever since I was a little kid, I have always wanted to become a baker. I would always be in the kitchen trying to find a new recepie to try. My parents finally decided to take me to a baking class, and I absolutely loved it! I kept going to the class every week. I then went to The Culinary Institution of America. I majored in baking and fine pastries. Soon after graduating, I set out to find a job. I have worked at several other bakeries, but I always felt called to open my own bakery. There was always something I didn't like about the other bakeries I had worked at. A few years after I had graduated, I started planning my business. After about 8 years after I had graduated college, there was grand opening of The Cupcake Connection. Ever since then, we have been growing and expanding.

What we Do

Here, at The Cupcake Connection, we try our best to to make your experience the best it could possibly be. We use the best ingredients and top-notch utensils to carefully construct whatever you order. We are careful and efficient with everyone's order. If there is ever a mistake with your order, we will happily make you a completely new cake and give you a discount on our next purchase. We want our customers to be completely satisfied in anything and everything they order. A special thanks goes out to all of our customers and our many supporters. Another special thanks goes out to all of our employees who work so hard at what they do.
